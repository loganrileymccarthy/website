Boletus edulis

MykoWeb

This is the prized king bolete, also known as porcini. It is one of several boletus species that grow in the coastal forests, associated mainly with Bishop pine and Monterey pine. I believe I have found both B. edulis and B. regineus in the same region of mixed forest, but I have had a hard time telling them apart. It is possible that the B. edulis and B. regineus photos on this site are all the same species, but the B. regineus specimens had significantly yellower pores and more of the reticulation on the stipes.
