Boletus regineus

MykoWeb

This mushroom is similar to Boletus edulis, and it can be quite difficult to tell the two apart. I'm currently treating any that I find with oak as B. regineus, and any with pine as B. edulis.

This 'queen bolete' is more stout and has a darker cap than the king. It tends to be associated with hardwoods, but apparently can be found with conifers as well. I have dried some flesh from the mushrooms in these photos, but have not eaten it yet.
