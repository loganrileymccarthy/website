Amanita calyptroderma

Wikipedia

One of the more rare amanitas in our area, this mushroom is rather striking. The deep brown caps are normally partially covered by a large, thick veil remnant, and the vulva is often visible and upright. It is a known edible, but could be easily confused with dark-colored Amanita phalloides (death cap) specimens.
